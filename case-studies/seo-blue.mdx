Crypto companies are notoriously hard to reach through traditional outbound. The space is full of scams and spam, so decision-makers have their guard up higher than any other vertical. Standard SEO agency outreach gets immediately deleted in crypto inboxes — prospects need sector-specific proof they can verify independently.

Generic SEO outreach without crypto-sector-specific proof points or independently verifiable ranking claims was producing no pipeline in a market where trust is the scarcest resource.

## The Solution

Prospect lists focused on legitimate crypto projects with real products, active communities, and organic search deficits. Messaging led with independently verifiable results: actual ranking improvements and traffic gains from other crypto clients that founders could check against publicly available data.

### Cold Email

Sequences led with sector-specific proof: ranking improvements and traffic gains for crypto clients, using metrics founders could verify independently. Enterprise infrastructure (2 USA IPs, 100 Outlook inboxes) penetrated aggressive crypto spam filters that block most vendor outreach.

### AI Cold Calling

Second touchpoint for high-value prospects who engaged with emails but didn't book. Technically informed calls referencing specific SEO gaps visible on the prospect's own site converted interest into meetings.

### Beyond the Meetings

- **Market Intelligence:** Prospect filtering revealed that DeFi protocols with comparable domain authority responded at the highest rates to audit-based messaging, refining SEO Blue's crypto sub-segment focus.
- **Pipeline Insurance:** AI calling caught high-value prospects who opened emails but didn't respond, converting email engagement into meetings through direct, technically informed conversations.
- **ICP Refinement:** Legitimate crypto projects with active communities but poor organic search showed the highest conversion, as they had the clearest ROI case for SEO investment.
- **Verifiable Proof Advantage:** Leading with independently verifiable ranking claims separated SEO Blue from dozens of generic agency pitches crypto founders receive weekly.
